# 🌱 NPKS Soil Analysis System

> **ITBI-Incubated Startup | 🥈 2nd Place — National Agriculture Hackathon**

A proprietary multi-element soil nutrient detection system that measures **Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K), and Sulfur (S)** levels in real-time — designed to bring affordable, instant soil testing directly to Indian farmers.

> ⚠️ **Note:** This is an active startup project under commercial incubation at iTBI, RVR & JC College of Engineering. Source code and circuit designs are proprietary and not publicly available.

## 🌾 The Problem We Solve

Indian farmers spend **₹500–₹2000 per soil test** and wait **3–7 days** for lab results.

By the time results arrive, the planting window may already be missed.

**Our solution delivers results in under 60 seconds — in the field — at a fraction of the cost.**

---

## 💡 What It Does

- ✅ Detects **4 key soil nutrients** — N, P, K, and S simultaneously
- ✅ **Instant results** — no lab required
- ✅ **Field deployable** — works anywhere, no internet needed
- ✅ **Low cost** — reusable hardware built for Indian farmers
- ✅ **Custom hardware** — built entirely from scratch, not off-the-shelf

---

## 🔧 Technical Highlights

- Custom **analog sensor array** designed from scratch
- Proprietary **signal conditioning circuits** for precise measurement
- Custom **PCB designed in EasyEDA** — multi-layer, probe form-factor
- Embedded firmware for real-time data processing
- Simple **LCD interface** accessible to non-technical farmers

> *Detailed technical specifications are confidential as part of the startup's IP.*

---

## 🌍 Impact & Vision

India has **140+ million farming households**. Most cannot afford regular soil testing.

Poor soil nutrition awareness leads to:
- Over-fertilization (wastes money, damages soil)
- Under-fertilization (reduces crop yield)
- Incorrect fertilizer type selection

**Our goal:** Put accurate soil intelligence in every farmer's hands.
